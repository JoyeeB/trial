Gaga's Meatball Gravy Sauce'

The secret to Lady Gaga's gravy sauce (which accompanies the Germanotta family's special meatball recipe) is a "ton of olive oil" and a "smack of garlic" crushed up in your own hands. Very DIY, as you would expect. Lady Gaga revealed her recipe during a radio interview with Z100 in 2011.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Mix all ingredients together carefully.

step 2

Sear meatballs in medium hot skillet.

step 3

Pour off excess oil add marinara sauce and simmer.

step 4

Cook spaghetti to al dente.

step 5

Toss meatballs and sauce with spaghetti.

step 6

Serve with grated parmesan cheese.

ingredients

    1 2/3 cups ciabatta (torn and soaked in 2/3 cups milk)
    1 egg
    ¼ cups grated parmesan cheese
    1/3 cup chopped fresh basil
    1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
    1 Tbs finely minced garlic
    1/3 cup minced onions
    ½ lb lean beef
    ½ lb lean pork
    1 Tbs extra virgin olive oil (extra)
    sea salt and fresh ground pepper
    4 cups of marinara sauce
