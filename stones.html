Stones' Hot Dog on the Rocks

According to the print at the top, “Mick Jagger invented the potatoes and franks; Charlie Watts added the beans.”

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

5 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare instant mashed potatoes, or boil and mash the potatoes. (Use milk and butter, making regular, every-day mashed potatoes.)

step 2

Cook the frankfurters according to the package directions and heat the baked beans.

step 3

On each plate, serve a mound of creamy mashed potatoes ringed by heated canned baked beans.

step 4

Over all the top of this, slice up the frankfurters in good-sized chunks.

ingredients

    10 frankfurters
    5 potatoes (enough to serve five
    1 large can baked beans
